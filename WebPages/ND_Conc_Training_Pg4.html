FACT: Athletes at every level under-report signs and symptoms of concussions.
Excuses for Not Reporting
  • Culture of toughness - "Just shake it off"
  • Concerns about letting down coaches and teammates
  • Fear of losing playing time
  • Concern that others may think you are faking or "flopping"
In Actuality
  • You are hurting your team by not being 100%.
  • This is your life! Do not risk a lifetime of physical impairment for any reason.
Know your body and the signs and symptoms.

If something doesn't feel right, do not risk it.
