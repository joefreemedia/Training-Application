This is a Myth

While many concussions are caused by a direct blow to the head, concussions can also occur when an indirect impact to the body transfers an impulsive force to the head resulting in sudden, violent movements to the brain.

This is a Myth

While many concussions are caused by a direct blow to the head, concussions can also occur when an indirect impact to the body transfers an impulsive force to the head resulting in sudden, violent movements to the brain.
